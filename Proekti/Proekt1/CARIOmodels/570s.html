CARIO 570S

Experience everyday drivability combined with racetrack performance in the CARIO 570S. This model is built for thrill and luxury, making it a perfect all-rounder hypercar.

Specifications

  • Engine: 3.8L Twin-Turbo V8
  • Horsepower: 562 HP
  • Top Speed: 204 mph
  • 0-60 mph: 3.1 seconds

Key Features

  • Advanced infotainment and audio system
  • Carbon-ceramic brakes for superior stopping power
  • Luxurious interior with Alcantara® and Nappa leather
  • Proactive Chassis Control for precision handling

Design

With a dynamic, refined exterior, the 570S radiates sophistication. The curved doors and sleek contours reduce drag, while a spacious interior ensures comfort, whether you’re on the road or the track.
