CARIO 540C

The CARIO 540C offers unparalleled efficiency and agility. Perfect for those looking to enter the hypercar world, it brings CARIO performance within reach.

Specifications

  • Engine: 3.8L Twin-Turbo V8
  • Horsepower: 533 HP
  • Top Speed: 199 mph
  • 0-60 mph: 3.4 seconds

Key Features

  • Efficient fuel management system
  • Lightweight, ergonomic seating
  • Dual-zone climate control for comfort
  • Hydraulic steering for precise maneuverability

Design

The 540C combines simplicity with functionality. Its flowing lines and integrated design elements reduce wind resistance and create a visually striking profile.
