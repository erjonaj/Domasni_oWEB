CARIO 650S

The CARIO 650S combines high performance and stunning design for a thrilling driving experience. Engineered with a twin-turbo V8 engine, it embodies power, speed, and precision.

Specifications

  • Engine: 3.8L Twin-Turbo V8
  • Horsepower: 641 HP
  • Top Speed: 207 mph
  • 0-60 mph: 2.9 seconds

Key Features

  • Active aerodynamics for enhanced performance
  • Advanced carbon-fiber monocoque construction
  • Lightweight sports exhaust system
  • Adaptive suspension for track and road

Design

The CARIO 650S is crafted with sleek, aerodynamic lines that optimize airflow and enhance handling. Every detail, from its distinctive LED headlights to its aerodynamic fins, is designed for peak performance.
